Mario Maker: Instructions

I Updated Controls for Moving Mario:
  • Cursor keys / 'W', 'A', 'S' 'D' or arrow keys to move.
  • Press 'S' or use the down arrow key to drop from semi-solid platforms aka the blue & orange platforms.
Level Editor Keys:
  • 0 (zero) - toggle level editor
  • 1-4 - switch tiles
  • E - Pick tile under cursor
  • 9 - Place Enemies - Goomba, Life Mushroom and Piranha plants

  • R - Reset level, can change the width and height of the level and clear the level.
